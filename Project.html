Food is more than just something we eat when we are hungry. It gives our body energy, helps us grow, keeps us healthy, and even affects our mood and concentration. Good nutrition is important for everyone, especially students and teenagers, because the body and brain are still developing.

A balanced diet includes carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water. Carbohydrates provide energy for daily activities.

Vitamins

Vitamins and minerals are needed in smaller amounts, but they are still very important. Vitamin C helps boost immunity, while calcium keeps bones and teeth strong. Eating colorful fruits and vegetables is one of the best ways to get these nutrients naturally.
>
Healthy eating does not mean giving up your favorite foods completely. It means eating everything in moderation and making smart choices most of the time.
